    Facilities

  • Classroom: Spacious, well ventilated, well-lit class rooms are for all departments.
  • Smart Classrooms: Two smart classrooms are with Audio-Visual teaching facilities.
  • Library: Our library is equipped with with around 8000 books of different titles and authors with required journals.
  • Laboratory: All Departments (ME, EE, CST, ETC) are well equipped with modern Laboratories for Practical Classes. Physics and Chemistry labs are also equipped with all apparatus and chemicals.
  • Computer Laboratory: State of the art Computer Laboratory with nearly 150 computers.
  • Seminar Room: A spacious AC language laboratory is with all equipments, computers & accessories and sound system.
  • Workshops: Workshops building is equipped with all machines and equipments.
  • We also have CNC M/C under the Mechanical Department along with one Robotics Laboratory under the Electrical Department. Our institution is now under WIFI connection & we also introduced the Green Campus Initiative in the year 2016.
